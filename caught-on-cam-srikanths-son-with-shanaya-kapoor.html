Srikanth's Son with Shanaya Kapoor

September 08, 2024

Article by Satya B Published on: 3:17 pm, 17 October 2023

Mohanlal is busy with his next pan-India film titled Vrushaba. The film also features a bunch of talented actors including the budding star kids. Tollywood actor Srikanth’s son Roshan will be part of this prestigious project, along with Sanjay Kapoor’s daughter Shanaya Kapoor.

It looks like both Roshan and Shanaya are going to be a pair in the film and share a great rapport even off the screen. Last night, the young couple were spotted in Mumbai, and the paps went crazy, capturing them in their cameras.

We can see Roshan and Shanaya smiling to the cameras and they make a good pair.

The shooting of the film is currently in progress in Mumbai. Producers for this pan-Indian project include Abhishek Vyas, Praveer Singh, Ekta Kapoor, and Shyam Sunder. Nanda Kishore is the film’s director.
