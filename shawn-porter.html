Shawn Porter was born in Akron, OH on October 27, 1987. American professional welterweight boxer who, as of 2014, is undefeated in pro matches and is the IBF World Welterweight Champion. He was Manny Pacquiao‘s sparring partner in preparation for Pacquiao’s fight against Miguel Cotto. As an amateur, he had a record of 276-14 and won the World Golden Gloves Championship in 2007.
