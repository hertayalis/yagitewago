WWE 2020 Slammy Awards Winners Live Coverage

June 13, 2024
blog

WWE brought back the Slammy Awards for 2020, a year that included a lot of unique circumstances. Let’s see who the big winners were. This is live coverage, SO KEEP REFRESHING.

R-Truth hosted the Slammy Awards, but when he showed up to the WWE Performance Center nobody was there. He said that the show must go on, and it did.

A lot of fans had questions about why WWE picked certain nominees. You can click here for Ringside News’ exclusive report on just how much input Vince McMahon had.

You can check out the categories, nominees, and winners below.

Shawn Michaels presented Male Superstar Of The Year:

  • Drew McIntyre – Winner
  • Roman Reigns
  • Randy Orton
  • Braun Strowman
  • Bray Wyatt/ The Fiend

Snooki presented Ring Gear of the Year

  • Charlotte Flair
  • Sasha Banks
  • Seth Rollins
  • The New Day – Winners
  • Bianca Belair
  • Shinsuke Nakamura
  • Carmella

Mick Foley presented Return of the Year

  • Edge – Winner
  • Roman Reigns
  • MVP
  • Goldberg
  • Sami Zayn

Elias Won Musical Performance of the Year presented by Method Man

Demi Burnette presented Rivalry of the Year

  • Seth Rollins vs. The Mysterio Family
  • Drew McIntyre vs. Randy Orton
  • Edge vs. Randy Orton – Winner
  • Sasha Banks vs. Bayley
  • R-Truth vs. The World
  • Lana vs. Announcer Tables

Trish Stratus presented Female Superstar of the Year

  • Asuka
  • Sasha Banks – Winner
  • Bayley
  • Becky Lynch
  • Charlotte Flair

Derek Baker presented Breakout Superstar of the Year

  • Dominik Mysterio
  • Bianca Belair
  • Otis
  • The Street Profits – Winners
  • Murphy

The Bump hosts gave out some awards

Social Media Superstar of the Year: Bayley

Trash Talker of the Year: Female: Lacey Evans, Male: Hurt Business

WWE Network Documentary of the Year: The Last Ride

Celebrity of the Year: Rob Gronkowski

Double Cross of the Year: Bayley

Referee of the Year: Charles Robinson (John Cone showed up and cursed a lot and then he showed up with a Ric Flair wig on trying to steal the award)

Ken Jeong presented Moment of the Year

  • The Undertaker’s Final Farewell: Survivor Series 2020 – Winner
  • Drew McIntyre defeats Brock Lesnar for the WWE Championship: WrestleMania 36
  • Becky Lynch announces her pregnancy: Raw, May 11, 2020
  • Edge returns in the Men’s Royal Rumble Match: Royal Rumble 2020
  • The New Day’s farewell address: SmackDown, Oct. 16, 2020
  • Roman Reigns and Paul Heyman unite: SmackDown, August 28, 2020
  • Bayley betrays Sasha Banks: SmackDown, Sept. 4, 2020
  • The New Day gets drafted to different brands: Raw, Oct. 12, 2020

Clark Duke presented Tag Team of the Year

  • The Golden Role Models
  • Nia Jax & Shayna Baszler
  • The New Day
  • The Street Profits – Winners
  • Shinsuke Nakamura & Cesaro

Rico and Raini Rodriguez presented Match of the Year

  • Undertaker vs. AJ Styles – Boneyard Match, WrestleMania 36 – Winner
  • The New Day vs. The Hurt Business – Raw Tag Team Championship Match: Raw, Nov. 16, 2020
  • Edge vs. Randy Orton – The Greatest Wrestling Match Ever: WWE Backlash 2020
  • Men’s Royal Rumble Match: Royal Rumble 2020
  • AJ Styles vs. Daniel Bryan – Intercontinental Championship Tournament Finals: SmackDown, June 12, 2020
  • Roman Reigns vs. Jey Uso – Universal Championship Hell in a Cell “I Quit” Match: WWE Hell in a Cell 2020
  • Sasha Banks vs. Bayley – SmackDown Women’s Championship Hell in a Cell Match: WWE Hell in a Cell 2020
  • AJ Styles vs. Sami Zayn vs. Jeff Hardy – Intercontinental Championship Triple Threat Ladder Match: WWE Clash of Champions 2020
  • Drew McIntyre vs. Roman Reigns: Survivor Series 2020
  • Becky Lynch vs. Asuka – Royal Rumble 2020
