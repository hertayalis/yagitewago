Yuzvendra Chahal and Dhanashree Verma had recently met South Indian superstar Yash and his wife Radhika Pandit.

While team India's leg-spinner Yuzvendra Chahal often makes headlines because of his reaction to wife Dhanashree Verma and her performances, this time, the choreographer had a new dance partner.

In a video posted by Indian batsman Shreyas Iyer, he and Dhanashree were seen doing the shuffle dance on SAINt JHN 'Roses' song.

The Delhi Capitals (DC) skipper captioned the video as, "Thinking on our feet" and tagged Dhanashree and Kish, a photographer and cinematographer - mainly associated with dance crew MJ5.

Recently, Chahal had taken to Instagram and shared a picture while showcasing his recent encounter with South Indian superstar Yash and his wife Radhika Pandit. 

The cricketer shared the picture where he along with his wife Dhanashree were seen striking a cool pose with Yash and Radhika. He captioned the post with a smiling emoticon that seems to justify his happiness after meeting the KGF actor. 

As for Shreyas Iyer, he along with Prithvi Shaw, Surya Kumar Yadav and Sachin Tendulkar's son Arjun were named 100 players for their probable players' camp by the Mumbai Cricket Association (MCA) for the Vijay Hazare Trophy.

The BCCI recently decided that the Vijay Hazare Trophy will be held as scheduled while the Ranji Trophy won't take place for the first time in 87 years.
