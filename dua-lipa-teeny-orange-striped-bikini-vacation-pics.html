See Dua Lipa Lounge in a Teeny Orange Striped Bikini

August 06, 2024
blog

Dua Lipa's enviable vacation style is back.

On Instagram today, the pop star shared a new bikini-filled photo dump from her recent sun-kissed getaway, as she returned to beachside dressing after her recent red-carpet appearances. One standout two-piece consists of a triangle string top and matching cheeky bottoms, featuring an orange, red, and taupe striped pattern. She accessorized the bright suit with an array of gold jewelry, including hoop earrings, a pendant necklace, and a belly chain with dainty floral charms, as well as gold-rimmed shades.

In some waterside snaps, the "Dance the Night" singer adds a coverup over the striped suit, layering on a long-sleeved orange minidress with ruched detailing and cutouts along the torso, while carrying a green rattan tote. She also styles some bikini tops with casual separates, pairing a white scooped-neck top with pinstripe shorts in one pic, and wearing a yellow button-down loose over a floral triangle top in another.

She captioned the post with the Albanian phrase, "A ma mire ktu a atje?" which translates to, "Is it better here or there?"

Earlier this week, the singer shared a series of pics where she posed on a rocky shore by the ocean while wearing a plunging backless update on the classic LBD, courtesy of Celine. She paired the wool minidress, which featured white trim embellished with glittering ivory sequins, with caramel-leather gladiator sandals, also by the French house. Her accessories included chunky gold hoop earrings, diamond earring cuffs, a delicate gold wristwatch, and textured gold rings.
